Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a childhood favorite for most. It is a simple yet tasty dish.
It just takes a few minutes to make and enjoy. So lets fire up that stove and lets get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Heat up pan
  2. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice
  3. Add cheese to non buttered side and stack bread slices with buttered sides out
  4. Place sandwhich on hot pan/griddle
  5. Cook each side for about 1 min
  6. Remove from pan to plate and cut diagonally then enjoy!
