Fried Rice

Description

A staple in Tex-mex cuisine the quesadilla is a versitile dish that can be customized as you wish. With endless combinations this dish will never be boring.
It just takes a few minutes to make and enjoy. So lets fire up that stove and lets get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • Optional: vegtables,meats,herbs of your choosing

Steps

  1. Heat up flat pan or griddle
  2. Place tortilla down on pan and let it heat up for 20 secs
  3. Flip tortilla to other side then top one side with cheese and any other ingredients
  4. Flip untopped side over the side with toppings
  5. Let it cook on one side for approx. 1 min
  6. Flip to the other side and let cook for approx. 1 min
  7. Remove from pan on to cutting board and cut into 4 pieces
  8. Enjoy right off cutting board or serve on plate
