Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a goto in Asian cuisine. It is a simple yet tasty dish.
It just takes a few minutes to make and enjoy. So lets fire up that stove and lets get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice (day old recommend but not required)
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegtables
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat up wok or deep pan on High and add 1 Tbsp oil
  2. add egg and scramble
  3. remove scrambled egg on to seperate plate
  4. add remaining oil to pan and add vegtables
  5. cook vegtables until soft then add rice
  6. mix until rice is soft then add soy sauce
  7. mix until soy sauce is incorporated
  8. Salt and pepper to taste
  9. Serve and enjoy
